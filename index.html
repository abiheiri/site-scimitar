I started with the superstrats but couldn't find guitars that met my standards so I decided to build my own.

I select premium hardware and woods such as purpleheart and wenge. My builds feature deep contours, no front fret markers, and utilize string ferrules instead of plates to enhance upper fret access.

I avoid the heavy lacquer found on mass-produced guitars so the neck finishing preserves the natural wood feel while providing just enough protection against moisture.

I use stainless steel frets for enhanced durability, and occasionally add unique touches such as glow inlays for distinctive visual appeal.
